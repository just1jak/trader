# Areas

Ongoing responsibilities with no defined end date.

## Typical Areas (customize to your life)

- Finances / Money Machine
- Health & Fitness
- Home / Homelab (Proxmox, Docker, etc.)
- Relationships / Family
- Career / Skill Development
- Spiritual / Personal Growth
- Community / Contribution

## Area Template

When defining an area, consider:
- Standard to maintain
- Regular check-ins or reviews
- Metrics or indicators of health
- Related projects that support this area
