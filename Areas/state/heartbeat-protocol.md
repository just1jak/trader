# Heartbeat Protocol

## Purpose
To ensure continuous alignment between the CEO (Hermes) and the Principal (User) across fragmented projects.

## Cadence
- **Morning Briefing:** State of the union, overnight runs, today's critical path.
- **End-of-Week Summary:** Shipped vs. Blocked, skill updates, memory pruning.
- **Ad-hoc:** High-priority alerts ("on fire").

## Structure
1. **Headline:** The primary status.
2. **Actions Taken:** What actually happened.
3. **Open Questions:** What is blocking progress.
4. **Next Steps:** The immediate sequence of moves.
