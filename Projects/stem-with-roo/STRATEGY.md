# STEM with Roo Strategy

## What Is It
Children's STEM education brand. Core products:
1. **Animated YouTube shorts** (15–60 seconds): Roo (character) explains electricity, circuits, magnetism, etc. Built with Remotion pipeline.
2. **Books** (24-page children's books): Roo's adventures with STEM concepts. First book "Roo's First Circuit Adventure" in progress.
3. **Social media**: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts (same content).

## Audience
Kids ages 6–10. Parents, homeschooling groups, STEM educators.

## Revenue Model
1. **YouTube**: Ad revenue (requires 10K subs + 4K watch hours to monetize)
2. **Books**: Amazon KDP paperback + Kindle + free PDF to newsletter subscribers
3. **Merchandise**: Branded items (shirts, hats, stickers) — future phase
4. **Patreon/Sponsorships**: Patrons get early book releases, Roo exclusive content
