# 75 Hard App Strategy

## Overview
The 75 Hard app supports the 75 Hard challenge, a 75-day mental toughness program by Andy Frisella. It tracks daily requirements to build discipline.

## MVP Features
- **Workouts**: Log two 45-minute workouts per day (one outdoor if possible). Track duration, type (e.g., cardio, strength), and location.
- **Water Intake**: Track 1 gallon (128 oz) of water consumption with progress bar and reminders.
- **Diet Adherence**: Log meals to ensure adherence to a chosen diet plan (no cheat meals or alcohol). Include photo uploads for meals.
- **Progress Photos**: Daily front, back, side photos with timestamps for visual transformation tracking.
- **Reading**: Log 10 pages of non-fiction reading per day. Integrate book suggestions or progress tracker for e-books.

## Core Mechanics
- Strict no-repeat days: Missing any task requires restarting from Day 1.
- Daily checklist with notifications.
- Progress dashboard showing streak, completion percentage.
- Community sharing (optional premium feature).

## Monetization
Freemium: Basic tracking free; premium for advanced analytics, custom plans, integration with wearables.
