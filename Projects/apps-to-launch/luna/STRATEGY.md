# Luna Period Tracking App

## Overview
Luna is a new mobile app for period tracking, focusing on cycle prediction, symptom logging, and women's health insights. Part of the iOS app portfolio alongside CleanStreak and 75 Hard.

## Core Strategy
- **MVP Focus**: Basic cycle tracking, reminders for periods/ovulation, symptom journal, privacy-first data storage (no unnecessary sharing).
- **Target Audience**: Women aged 18-45 seeking simple, ad-free tracking; expand to fertility planning and health integration.
- **Monetization**: Freemium model with premium subscriptions ($2.99/mo for advanced predictions, export, integrations). Align with quick App Store release like CleanStreak (May 2026 window).
- **Differentiation**: Emphasis on user privacy, customizable insights, integration with Apple Health/Wearables.
- **Launch Path**: User-provided details → wireframes → TestFlight → App Store. P1 priority for revenue pipeline.

## Key Decisions
- Platform: iOS first (fits portfolio).
- Regulatory: HIPAA/GDPR compliant for health data; review privacy policy early.
- Timeline: MVP in 4-6 weeks post-details; launch by June 2026.
