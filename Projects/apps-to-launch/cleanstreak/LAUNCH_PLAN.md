# CleanStreak: 2-Week Launch Plan (May 12–25, 2026)

## Week 1: App Store + Marketing Foundation (May 12–18)

### Day 1 (Mon, May 12)
**Goal**: App Store submission + marketing assets finalized
- [ ] **App Store**: Submit current TestFlight build to App Store (expects 3–5 day review)
  - Screenshot captions ready
  - Privacy policy + terms linked
  - Keywords optimized (habit, streak, tracking, sobriety, wellness)
  - Estimated review complete: May 15–17
- [ ] **Social**: Finalize 8 TikTok scripts (30–60 sec each) covering:
  1. "What is a streak?" (hook: why tracking matters)
  2. "Sobriety streaks work" (testimonial angle)
  3. "Beat your record" (gamification hook)
  4. "Recovery is daily" (emotional angle)
  5. "Track anything" (flexibility: exercise, meditation, etc.)
  6. "One day at a time" (motivational)
  7. "Join 50K others" (FOMO/social proof — use lower number if false; verify)
  8. "Download CleanStreak today" (CTA)
- [ ] **Website**: cleanstreak.club landing page live with:
  - Download button → App Store link (when live)
  - Feature summary (3–4 bullets)
  - Social proof section (placeholder for user testimonials)
  - Email signup (ConvertKit or similar)

### Day 2 (Tue, May 13)
**Goal**: Paid ads + email list warming
- [ ] **Facebook/Instagram Ads**: Set up Meta Business account + 2 ad campaigns
  - Campaign A: "Habit Tracker" (general audience: 25–45, wellness + sobriety interests)
  - Campaign B: "Sobriety Support" (specific audience: sobriety + recovery groups)
  - Budget: $250/week ($35/day), $1,000 reserved for May 12–31
  - Lookalike audiences: Build from existing waitlist (if available)
- [ ] **Email**: Export existing waitlist (from previous marketing) and segment by intent:
  - "Sobriety focus" (30 people) → send sobriety-angle email
  - "General wellness" (20 people) → send generic habit-tracking email
  - Template: Launch announcement + exclusive early-bird offer (7 days free, then $4.99/month)

### Day 3 (Wed, May 14)
**Goal**: TikTok/Instagram content production starts
- [ ] **Video Production**: Film 2–3 TikTok scripts (use iPhone + simple ring light)
  - TikTok #1: "What is a streak?" (60 sec, hook in first 3 sec)
  - TikTok #2: "Sobriety streaks work" (45 sec, testimonial-style)
  - Post-production: CapCut (add text overlays, trending audio)
  - Upload to drafts (hold for coordinated launch May 17)
- [ ] **Twitter/X**: Set up posting schedule (3x/week):
  - Monday: Motivation quote + streak psychology
  - Wednesday: Feature highlight + tip
  - Friday: Community win or testimonial

### Day 4 (Thu, May 15)
**Goal**: Monitor App Store review + prepare launch day
- [ ] **App Store Status**: Check review progress (should be under review or approved by now)
  - If rejected: Fix issue + resubmit same day
  - If approved: Prepare announcement posts
- [ ] **Reddit**: Post in r/addiction, r/stopdrinking, r/nosurf, r/meditation (1 post per subreddit)
  - Angle: "Built a streak tracker because I couldn't find a good one—now using it myself. Feedback welcome."
  - Link to cleanstreak.club
  - Monitor comments for questions/feedback

### Day 5 (Fri, May 16)
**Goal**: All hands on social media launch
- [ ] **Launch Day (if App Store approved)**:
  - 9 AM: Tweet + Instagram story announcement
  - 10 AM: TikTok upload (all 3 videos scheduled simultaneously)
  - 11 AM: Send email to waitlist (early-bird offer)
  - 2 PM: Post to Reddit communities (if not done Thu)
  - 5 PM: Verify links work, monitor engagement
- [ ] **Tracking**: Create spreadsheet to log:
  - Instagram Reels views/engagement (daily)
  - TikTok video views (daily)
  - Email open rate + click rate
  - App Store ranking (daily)
  - Paid ads ROAS (return on ad spend)

### Day 6–7 (Sat–Sun, May 17–18)
**Goal**: Content amplification + community engagement
- [ ] **Community Engagement**:
  - Reply to all Reddit comments + DMs within 4 hours
  - Like + comment on related wellness/sobriety posts (hashtag searches)
  - Join 2–3 Reddit community AMAs or discussions (provide value, link only if asked)
- [ ] **Content**: Film + post 2 more TikToks from script backlog
- [ ] **Analytics**: Weekly review (engagement, signups, installs) by Sunday 6 PM

## Week 2: Paid Ads + Growth Hacking (May 19–25)

### Day 8 (Mon, May 19)
**Goal**: Optimize paid ads + expand organic reach
- [ ] **Ads Optimization**: Review Facebook/Instagram campaign performance
  - Pause underperforming ad (ROAS < 1.5x)
  - Increase budget on winners (ROAS > 3x) by $10–15/day
  - Test new ad creative: user testimonial video (if available)
  - A/B test copy: "Habit Tracker" vs. "Sobriety Streak Tracker" (same image, different headline)
- [ ] **YouTube Shorts**: Post best TikTok videos to YouTube Shorts (auto-generates from TikTok if possible)
  - Add YouTube-specific CTAs: "Subscribe for more"
- [ ] **Newsletter Partnerships**: Reach out to 5 wellness/sobriety newsletters
  - Pitch: "In exchange for mentioning CleanStreak, I'll feature you in our app's featured resources section"
  - Example targets: ThisNation, The Recovery Roundup, Sober Powered

### Day 9 (Tue, May 20)
**Goal**: Press + influencer outreach
- [ ] **Influencer Outreach**: Contact 10 micro-influencers in sobriety/wellness space (10K–100K followers)
  - Pitch: Free app access + offer to promo code (give them unique code to track conversions)
  - Template: "Hey [name], I built a streak tracker for [niche]. Thought you'd like it. Happy to exchange a promo code if you'd mention it."
- [ ] **Press**: Post to Product Hunt (if haven't already)
  - Title: "CleanStreak – Habit & Sobriety Streak Tracker"
  - Maker comment with story + ask for feedback
  - Goal: Top 5 in category for day

### Day 10 (Wed, May 21)
**Goal**: User retention + referral setup
- [ ] **In-App Referral**: Add referral feature (if not already there)
  - Share code: 7-day free trial for both referrer + new user
  - Implement tracking: referral source logged in DB
- [ ] **Onboarding Email**: Set up 5-email sequence for new users (auto-triggered)
  - Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + setup help
  - Email 2 (Day 3): "How's your streak? Tips for success"
  - Email 3 (Day 7): "1-week milestone! Here's what's next"
  - Email 4 (Day 14): "Join 50K users tracking streaks" (social proof)
  - Email 5 (Day 30): Testimonial + upsell to yearly plan
- [ ] **Community**: Create private Discord or Slack channel for paid users
  - Invite early adopters + social media followers
  - Daily motivation post + streak milestone celebrations

### Day 11 (Thu, May 22)
**Goal**: Expand creator content + partnerships
- [ ] **Long-Form Content**: Write 2 blog posts for cleanstreak.club
  - Post 1: "Why Streaks Work: The Psychology of Daily Habits"
  - Post 2: "From Sobriety to Fitness: 5 Habits Worth Tracking"
  - SEO: Target keywords like "habit tracker," "sobriety tracker," "streak app"
  - Publish + submit to Reddit, Twitter communities
- [ ] **Partnerships**: Follow up with Day 9 influencer outreach
  - Check for responses, send reminder to non-respondents
  - Send promo codes to anyone who expressed interest

### Day 12 (Fri, May 23)
**Goal**: Mid-launch metrics review + next week planning
- [ ] **Analytics Dashboard**: Compile week 2 metrics
  - Total downloads (estimate from App Store page if possible)
  - Paid conversions (goal: 25–50 by end of week 2)
  - Paid ads ROAS (target: 2.0x or higher)
  - Email signups (from landing page)
  - Social media followers gained (TikTok, Instagram, Twitter)
  - Referral signups (if enabled)
- [ ] **Planning**: Based on metrics, decide what to double down on:
  - What ads are working? Increase budget.
  - What creators/communities are engaging? Target more.
  - What's underperforming? Kill it or pivot.
- [ ] **Outreach**: Send thank-you DM to top 10 referrers + micro-influencers who posted

### Day 13–14 (Sat–Sun, May 24–25)
**Goal**: Consolidate + plan weeks 3–4
- [ ] **Community Building**: Host live Q&A or AMA in Discord (Saturday 2 PM)
  - Answer questions about streak psychology, habit formation, sobriety
  - Record and post to YouTube Shorts + TikTok (turns 30 min into 4–5 short clips)
- [ ] **Content Factory**: Batch-create next week's posts (12 TikToks, 8 Instagram Reels, 6 tweets)
  - Use templates + editing software (CapCut, Adobe Express)
  - Schedule via Buffer or Later (post 3x/day Mon–Fri)
- [ ] **Wrap-up**: Email report to yourself summarizing:
  - What worked (keep doing)
  - What flopped (kill or redesign)
  - Metrics vs. targets
  - Next priorities (weeks 3–4)
