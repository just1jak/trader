# CleanStreak Strategy

## What Is It
Habit tracker app (iOS, rebranded from DryStreak). Tracks streaks for any habit (sobriety, exercise, meditation, etc.). Live on TestFlight.

## Revenue Model
Subscription: $4.99/month or $39.99/year. In-app purchase for streak recovery feature.

## Current Status
- **Code**: Feature-complete (streak restoration merged and tested)
- **TestFlight**: Live with early users
- **Marketing**: Materials drafted (market analysis, social playbook, ad guide)
- **Website**: cleanstreak.club

## Revenue Targets
- 100 paid subscribers by June 30 → $500/month MRR
- 500 paid subscribers by Sept 30 → $2,500/month MRR

## Next Phase: Launch + Growth
1. Push to App Store (review + approval expected 3–5 days)
2. Execute social media playbook (TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, Twitter)
3. Paid ads on Facebook/Instagram ($500/month initial spend)
4. Partner marketing with sobriety/wellness communities

## Success Metrics
- Install rate target: 1,000 downloads in first 30 days
- Conversion rate target: 5–10% paid (50–100 paid subscribers)
- Retention: 30-day active users ≥ 30% of installs
