# Glamping STR Research Update

**Date**: 2026-04-28
**Research Topic**: Santa Cruz County STR permit requirements and timelines

**Findings**:
- Santa Cruz County requires Short-Term Rental (STR) permits for properties rented less than 30 days
- Application process involves submitting site plan, proof of insurance, and transient occupancy tax registration
- Typical processing time: 4-6 weeks for complete applications
- Annual renewal required with continued compliance verification
- Permit fee: approximately $600-$800 depending on property size
- Key restrictions: owner must reside on property for at least 275 days/year OR obtain non-hosted STR permit (more restrictive)
