# Glamping STR Strategy: APNs 086-631-14 + 086-521-02

## Parcels
- **086-631-14** – 9.949 ac, zoning SU (Special Use). Primary glamping target.
- **086-521-02** – 1.492 ac, zoning RA. 20604 Little Basin Rd, Boulder Creek. Residence/access parcel.

The two parcels are part of a single purchase. RA (-02) is materially more permissive than SU (-14) for residential and STR uses, which affects which activities go on which parcel.

## Objective
Pay off the property through a scaled revenue model, moving from zero-capital primitive camping to high-margin glamping.

## The Ladder (Phase Execution)
1. **Phase 1: Primitive (Immediate)**
   - Model: Private Land Sharing (Hipcamp/Airbnb).
   - Infrastructure: Zero. Guests bring everything.
   - Goal: Immediate cash flow, zero regulatory footprint.
2. **Phase 2: Enhanced Primitive (Revenue Growth)**
   - Model: Rental Equipment.
   - Infrastructure: Non-permanent canvas bell tents.
   - Goal: Increase nightly rate without triggering "permanent structure" codes.
3. **Phase 3: Low-Impact Utilities (Value Add)**
   - Model: Amenity-driven.
   - Infrastructure: Composting toilets, off-grid solar, designated fire rings.
   - Goal: Move into "Glamping" territory while staying within "Special Use" limits.
4. **Phase 4: Full Glamping (The End State)**
   - Model: Luxury Experience.
   - Infrastructure: Platforms, permanent structures, professional utilities.
   - Goal: Maximum revenue per site.
